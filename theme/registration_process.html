REGISTRATION PROCEDURE

Ø  To get registered, student have to fill the application form provided along with Information Brochure.

Ø  Students can buy the Information Brochure from any of the Authorised Learning Centre (ALC) and register in any of our DIGITAL Computer Authorised Learning Center.

Ø  DIGITAL COMPUTER provides regular and University courses (only at selected ALCs).

Ø  The ALC name and code are to be mentioned in the application form at the time of admission.

Ø  The total fee has to be paid at the time of registration.

Enclosures with the application form.

1.      Two recent photograph one passport size and one stamp size.

2.      Attested Photo copy of last educational qualification.

3.      Category Certificate (ST/SC/Handicapped) to be submitted to get relaxation in the fee as per the M.S. Baijnath fee structure.

 

COURSE STRUCTURE

Considering the requirements of the aspirants, courses can be classified into

(i)                 Certificate courses (ii) Diploma/Advance Diploma courses.

Former is basically designed for the beginners where as the latter are for those who aspire to become high quality computer professionals.

 

Certificate Courses

·         These courses have been specifically designed to meet the current requirements of the IT Industry.

·         The Certificate course is basically for the beginners who desire to start their careers in computers.

·         M.S. Baijnath has presented this course with nominal charges so that every section of the society can easily

·         afford to learn the basics of computer & get benefitted.

Diploma/Advance Diploma Courses

·         To prepare a new highly qualified breed of computer professionals "Digital Computer A Unit of Digital computer Pvt. Ltd." has designed

·         its diploma courses. These courses have been well thought-out to meet the requirements of the fast growing IT industry

·         in which Internet Programming, Advanced Programming, Accountancy, Desk Top Publishing are being covered.

·         Students can get benefitted from a well premeditated course curriculum that matches the modern industry

·         needs and emerges as a well skilled IT professional.
